FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI meal scanning work?

Simply take a photo of your meal. Our AI analyzes the image and estimates the vitamins and nutrients present based on the ingredients it identifies.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → VitaGlow. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

Is my health data safe?

Yes. We do not sell or share your personal health data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How accurate is the vitamin tracking?

VitaGlow uses AI to estimate nutrient content based on visual analysis. Results are approximate and intended as a helpful guide, not a medical diagnosis.
