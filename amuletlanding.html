FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a widget to my Home Screen?

Long-press on your Home Screen, tap the “+” button, search for “Amulet”, and choose your preferred widget size. Then configure your symbol inside the app.

Which protection symbols are available?

Evil Eye (Nazar), Hamsa, Eye of Horus, Triquetra, Witches Knot, Bindrune, Pentacle, and Unicursal Hexagram — each with multiple artistic variations.

Can I customize the widget background?

Yes! Choose from 20+ background color presets or use the custom color picker to match your wallpaper.

How many widgets can I create?

Unlimited widget configurations across Small, Medium, and Large sizes.
